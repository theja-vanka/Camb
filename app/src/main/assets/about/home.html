CAMBRIDGE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY


Cambridge Institute of Technology is an offshoot of prestigious Umesh Education Trust with sprawling campus around. The Institution is approved by All India council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and permanently affiliated to VTU, Belagavi and recognized by government of Karnataka. Mr. D.K.Mohan, former President of the City Municipal Council, Krishnarajapuram, Bangalore is the Chairman of Cambridge Group of Institutions. Today, four decades later, Umesh Education Trust through Cambridge Institute of Technology (CiTech), is in the forefront among providers of quality education in the State of Karnataka. Ideally located on a verdant ten acres overlooking Krishnarajapuram, and 200 mts off old Madras Road, the college is accessible to all the important areas of Bangalore. The International Technology Park and Bio-Tech park from esteemed neighbours to the Picturesque Campus forms the natural choice for students pursuing knowledge in an environment conducive to both their internal as well as external harmony and growth.

Department of Placements & HRD provides its students the best in life, with HRD center functioning in the campus under the able administration of Prof. Guru Charan Singh. The department provides the many trainings programs on domain specific courses, placement training. Almost 100% of eligible students got placed. VISION

To impart aspiring students with Quality Education in Engineering, Technology & Management thus enabling them to acquire necessary leadership skills & become competent enough to face the challenges in the society on par with global standards

MISSION

To build, establish, equip & implement a state of the art global standard infrastructural facility to impart high quality education in the areas of various programs in engineering, technology and management to produce personnel with desired level of skills and education combined with moral and ethical values which intern transform students into responsible citizens who are self-reliant and confident to meet the global requirement for the future.
